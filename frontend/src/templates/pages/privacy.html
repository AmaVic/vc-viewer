Privacy Policy

Transparency

At VC Viewer, we take your privacy seriously. Our approach is simple: we collect absolutely no data from our users. This policy outlines our commitment to protecting your privacy and explains how we handle your information.

Data Collection

We maintain a strict no-data-collection policy:

  • No analytics tools (including Google Analytics)
  • No tracking scripts
  • No user behavior monitoring
  • No personal information collection
  • No cookies of any kind

Server Logs

Our server maintains minimal logs for operational and debugging purposes only. In production, these logs only record:

  • The requested page or resource
  • The HTTP status code of the response
  • The timestamp of the request

Example log entry when viewing the homepage:

GET "/" 200 0.123

These logs:

  • Are automatically rotated and deleted after 7 days
  • Are never used for analytics or tracking
  • Do not contain any personal identifiable information
  • Are only used for debugging and maintaining service quality

Changes to Policy

Any changes to this privacy policy will be posted on this page. As we do not collect any user data or contact information, we cannot notify users directly of changes.
