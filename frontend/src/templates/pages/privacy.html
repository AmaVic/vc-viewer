Overview

At VC Viewer, we take your privacy seriously. Our approach is simple: we collect absolutely no data from our users. This policy outlines our commitment to protecting your privacy and explains how we handle your information.

Data Collection

We maintain a strict no-data-collection policy:

  • No analytics tools (including Google Analytics)
  • No tracking scripts
  • No user behavior monitoring
  • No personal information collection
  • No cookies of any kind

Our server maintains minimal logs for operational and debugging purposes only. In production, these logs only record:

  • The requested page or resource
  • The HTTP status code of the response
  • The timestamp of the request

Example log entry when viewing the homepage:

2025-01-25 01:06:18 [INFO] Starting server at http://127.0.0.1:8080
2025-01-25 01:06:25 [INFO] GET /viewer HTTP/1.1 200 0.680000
2025-01-25 01:06:25 [INFO] GET /frontend/src/themes/UniversityDegreeCredential/modern/modern.css HTTP/1.1 304 1.520000
2025-01-25 01:06:27 [INFO] GET /docs HTTP/1.1 200 3.886000

These logs:

  • Are not stored persistently across server runs
  • Are not used for analytics or tracking
  • Cannot be used to identify individual users or their activities
Note: We believe that the best way to protect user data is to not collect it in the first place.

Credential Handling

All credential processing happens entirely in your browser:

  • Your credentials never leave your device
  • All rendering and processing is done client-side
  • No data is transmitted to our servers
  • No credential information is stored

Transparency

We believe in complete transparency:

  • Our code is open source and available for inspection
  • We use no third-party services that could collect user data
  • We maintain a simple, static web application

For information about our cookie policy, please visit our Cookie Policy page.
