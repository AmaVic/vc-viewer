About VC Viewer

Our Mission

VC Viewer is an open-source tool designed to make W3C Verifiable Credentials more accessible and visually appealing. Our mission is to provide a simple, secure, and beautiful way to visualize digital credentials.

Key Features

Privacy First

All processing happens in your browser. Your credentials never leave your device.

Beautiful Themes

Multiple themes for different credential types, with more being added regularly.

Open Source

Fully open source and free to use. Contributions are welcome!

Export Options

Export your visualized credentials as images or PDFs.

About the Author

VC Viewer is developed by Amaury Vanderborght, a researcher and software engineer passionate about decentralized identity, privacy, and web technologies. His research interests include:

  • Decentralized Identity and Self-Sovereign Identity (SSI)
  • Privacy-Preserving Technologies
  • Web3 and Blockchain Technologies
  • Zero-Knowledge Proofs
