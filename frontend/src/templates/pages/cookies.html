Cookie Policy

Overview

At VC Viewer, we believe in complete transparency and user privacy. This cookie policy is simple: we do not use any cookies or similar tracking technologies.

No Cookies Policy

We maintain a strict no-cookies policy:

  • No essential cookies
  • No preference cookies
  • No analytics cookies
  • No marketing cookies
  • No tracking technologies of any kind

Our application runs entirely in your browser, and we do not need to store any information about your visit or preferences.

Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party services that might set cookies or track users. This includes:

  • No analytics services
  • No advertising networks
  • No social media plugins
  • No external content delivery networks that track users

Changes to Policy

Any changes to this cookie policy will be posted on this page. As we do not use cookies or collect any user data, we cannot notify users directly of changes.

For more information about how we handle your privacy, please visit our Privacy Policy page.
