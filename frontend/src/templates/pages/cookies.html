Overview

This Cookie Policy explains how VC Viewer handles browser cookies. Our policy is simple: we don't use any cookies at all.

No Cookies Used

We have deliberately chosen not to use any cookies in our application:

  • No session cookies
  • No tracking cookies
  • No third-party cookies
  • No analytics cookies
Note: Our application is designed to function completely without the need for cookies, ensuring maximum privacy for our users.

Privacy Commitment

Our no-cookie policy is part of our broader commitment to user privacy. We believe in:

  • Respecting user privacy
  • Minimizing data collection
  • Maintaining transparency

For more information about how we protect your privacy, please visit our Privacy Policy page.
